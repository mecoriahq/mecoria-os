Strict Editorial QA Workflow

1. Read the video-specific Script and SEO outputs.
2. Verify the narration word-count gate.
3. Evaluate hook strength and first-120-word value.
4. Check that the introduction advances rather than repeats the hook.
5. Evaluate the cause-and-effect narrative spine.
6. Evaluate specificity without rewarding invented precision.
7. Evaluate repetition and AI-style documentary filler.
8. Evaluate title, thumbnail text, and title-thumbnail synergy.
9. Reject estimated chapter timestamps.
10. Apply all critical score thresholds.
11. Return structured issues and regeneration-ready recommendations.
12. Approve only when every critical editorial gate passes.
