# Rise Dossier Claim Quarantine Policy

## Rule

A rejected individual claim does not automatically reject the entire
video research dossier.

Blocked claims are retained in the claims ledger for audit history but
cannot be referenced by the script.

## Continuation Gate

Rise Dossier may continue to script production when all conditions pass:

- at least 15 claims are approved
- approved claim coverage is at least 60 percent
- every blocked claim remains quarantined
- script validation rejects any reference to a blocked claim

If these thresholds are not met, the system requests targeted factual
research rather than weakening the evidence standard.

## Recovery

Claims ledgers produced under the legacy all-or-nothing policy are
invalidated automatically and rebuilt with the current policy.
