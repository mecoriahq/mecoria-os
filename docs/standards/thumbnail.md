# Mecoria Thumbnail Standard

Version: 1.0

---

## Purpose

Define the visual rules for all YouTube thumbnails.

---

## Goals

- High CTR
- Mobile readability
- One clear story
- One dominant subject
- Strong curiosity

---

## Rules

- One primary subject
- Large readable composition
- Clean background
- Clear focal point
- High visual contrast
- Minimal distractions

---

## Text

If text is used:

- Maximum 3–5 words
- Large font
- High contrast
- Supports the image
- Never explains the entire story

---

## Forbidden

- Multiple competing subjects
- Clutter
- Tiny unreadable objects
- Weak contrast
- Generic stock-photo appearance
