# Hiddenova Intro / Outro Standard v1

## Purpose

Hiddenova videos should not feel like raw narration dropped onto visuals.

Every public video needs a reusable opening and closing structure.

---

## Opening Structure

The video should not start with a generic "Welcome to Hiddenova" line.

Correct structure:

1. Cold open: 5-8 seconds of curiosity / tension / hidden-system framing
2. Hiddenova context line: short brand/context sentence
3. Micro-summary: 1-2 sentences explaining what the viewer will understand

Bad opening:

"Welcome to Hiddenova. Today we are talking about returns."

Good opening:

"Every returned package looks simple. But behind it is a hidden system of warehouses, scanners, resale markets, and decisions most customers never see. Welcome to Hiddenova. In this video, we follow what really happens after you send something back."

---

## Outro Structure

Every video should close with a short CTA.

The CTA should be calm, not salesy.

Recommended ending:

"If you want more documentaries about the hidden systems behind everyday life, subscribe to Hiddenova. And tell us in the comments which system we should uncover next."

---

## Rules

- Keep intro short.
- Keep outro short.
- Avoid loud YouTuber energy.
- Keep serious documentary tone.
- Do not ask for like/subscribe before delivering value.
- Use the same voice model and voice as the main narration.
- Intro/outro must be generated by the pipeline, not manually edited.
- Reusable standard must apply to future Hiddenova videos.
