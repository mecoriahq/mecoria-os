# Mecoria Image Generation Standard

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

Owner: Mecoria OS

Last Updated: 2026-07-07

---

# Purpose

This document defines the official image generation standards used across every Mecoria Media brand.

All image-related AI agents must follow these rules.

These standards are provider-independent.

This document is the single source of truth for image quality inside Mecoria OS.

---

# Applies To

- Visual Brief Agent
- Image Prompt Agent
- Image Generation Agent
- QA Agent

---

# General Principles

Images must:

- Look realistic
- Feel cinematic
- Look premium
- Be timeless
- Be believable
- Tell one clear story
- Immediately communicate the video's main idea

Images must never resemble cheap AI art.

---

# Storytelling

Every image must answer these questions within one second:

- What is happening?
- Why is it interesting?
- What emotion should the viewer feel?

If the story is unclear, the image fails.

---

# Composition

Rules:

- One dominant subject
- Clear focal point
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Clean negative space
- Mobile-first readability
- Balanced composition

The primary subject should occupy approximately 30–40% of the frame.

Avoid visual clutter.

---

# Subject

The subject must:

- Be instantly recognizable
- Be the first thing the eye notices
- Support the story
- Never compete with secondary objects

---

# Background

Backgrounds should:

- Support the subject
- Add depth
- Feel believable
- Never distract
- Reinforce the story

Backgrounds should not contain unnecessary objects.

---

# Lighting

Preferred lighting:

- Cinematic
- Documentary
- Natural
- High contrast
- Motivated light sources

Avoid:

- Flat lighting
- Unrealistic glow
- Overexposure
- Artificial-looking shadows

---

# Color

Preferred palette:

- Deep blacks
- Rich contrast
- Natural color grading
- Controlled saturation
- Cinematic color balance

Avoid:

- Neon colors
- Oversaturation
- Washed-out colors
- Random color shifts

---

# Realism

Images must never resemble:

- CGI
- Cartoon
- Anime
- Plastic-looking people
- Low-quality AI art
- Unrealistic physics

Everything should feel like a real photograph.

---

# People

Only include people when they improve storytelling.

Rules:

- Natural poses
- Natural facial expressions
- Realistic proportions
- Minimal crowding

Avoid unnecessary people.

---

# Documentary Style

Preferred visual style:

- Editorial photography
- Documentary realism
- National Geographic quality
- Netflix documentary quality
- Premium commercial photography

---

# Text

Never generate:

- Titles
- Logos
- Watermarks
- Captions
- Signatures
- UI elements
- Fake labels
- Fake road signs

Text will always be added later by design tools.

---

# Thumbnail Readability

Every image must remain readable at YouTube thumbnail size.

Requirements:

- One dominant subject
- Strong silhouette
- Clear lighting
- Minimal distractions
- High contrast

The viewer should understand the image in less than one second.

---

# Forbidden

Never generate:

- Fake logos
- Fake text
- Watermarks
- Broken anatomy
- Extra limbs
- Random objects
- Distorted vehicles
- Unrealistic perspective
- Excessive visual clutter

---

# Continuous Improvement

Every production review should improve this document.

When a recurring issue is discovered:

1. Update this standard.
2. Update the related AI agent.
3. Document the change.
