# Mecoria Vision

## Vision

Become one of the world's leading AI-native companies by building businesses that operate through intelligent systems instead of manual processes.

## Long-Term Vision

Create a global ecosystem where every Mecoria business unit is powered by Mecoria OS, allowing media, commerce, software, sports and travel businesses to scale with minimal operational overhead.

## Future State

- AI manages repetitive operations.
- Humans focus on strategy, creativity and innovation.
- Every business unit shares the same operating system.
- Mecoria becomes a global AI-first holding company.
