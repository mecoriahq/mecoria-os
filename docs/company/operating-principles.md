# Mecoria Operating Principles

## 1. Architecture Before Code

Every system must be designed before implementation.

---

## 2. Documentation Before Implementation

Document important decisions before writing code.

---

## 3. Build Once, Reuse Everywhere

Avoid duplicate logic.
Create reusable components whenever possible.

---

## 4. Automation by Default

Every repetitive task should become an automated workflow.

---

## 5. AI is the First Team Member

Every business process should first be evaluated for AI automation before assigning human effort.

---

## 6. Standardization

Use common structures, naming conventions and workflows across all business units.

---

## 7. Measure Everything

Every important process should produce measurable data.

---

## 8. Continuous Improvement

Every sprint should improve the system, even if only by a small amount.

---

## 9. Security by Design

Protect credentials, customer data and company knowledge from the beginning.

---

## 10. Long-Term Thinking

Every decision should support the long-term scalability of Mecoria OS.
